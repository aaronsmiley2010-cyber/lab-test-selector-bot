Railway offers $5 of free credit each month - perfect for small apps!

### Step-by-Step Deployment

1. **Create a Railway account**: https://railway.app/
   - Sign up with GitHub

2. **Deploy from GitHub**:
   - Click "New Project"
   - Select "Deploy from GitHub repo"
   - Choose repository: `lab-test-selector-bot`
   - Select branch: `claude/create-page-011CV4wHnCmFw66F1CBenEN8`

3. **Railway auto-detects Python**:
   - It will automatically find your app
   - No additional configuration needed!

4. **Generate Domain**:
   - Go to "Settings" tab
   - Under "Domains", click "Generate Domain"
   - Your PIMS will be at: `https://your-app.up.railway.app/pims/`
